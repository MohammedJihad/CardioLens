Plain-language box

What this means

A higher model score means this input pattern looks more like the historical research patterns the model associates with heart disease in its training data. It does not describe you. To understand your own health, talk to a qualified clinician.

Can / cannot cards

What it can do

  • Show how one frozen model scores research-style input patterns.
  • Explain which inputs moved a given score, within the model.
  • Demonstrate that performance drops on unseen cohorts.

What it cannot do

  • Make any claim about a real person's health.
  • Give medical advice or a clinical recommendation.
  • Promise its probabilities hold outside this research data.

Accessibility & contrast

Body text meets WCAG AA in both modes. Teal text on white uses #277A6F (not the #2F8F83 accent) precisely so links and labels clear the 4.5:1 bar; the brighter accent is used only for non-text marks where 3:1 applies.

Dark mode mirrors this: ink #E8EAED (~14:1) and muted #9AA2AD (~7:1) on base #0E1117; teal #46A89B is text-safe on the dark base. Focus rings use a dedicated #3E63B8 / #7CA0F0 that clears 3:1 on every background. Reduced motion is honored; targets are ≥44px; color is never the only signal.
